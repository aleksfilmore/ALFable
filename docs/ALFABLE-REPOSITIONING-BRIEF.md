## 2. Background

ALFable is the independent practice of Alexandru Filip.

Alexandru has more than 23 years of experience split across two complementary environments:

- senior work in leading advertising agencies;
- European and global leadership in communications, brand, and digital strategy inside complex international organizations;
- more recently, hands-on independent work creating brands, websites, applications, software, content systems, and practical digital products.

The commercial focus is shifting away from classic corporate consulting toward:

- Romanian founders and entrepreneurs creating new businesses;
- owner-led SMEs that have outgrown their current identity or market position;
- lifestyle, fashion, interiors, hospitality, wellness, and other premium-oriented brands;
- businesses seeking to move from a mainstream or functional perception into a more desirable premium segment;
- founders who need one senior partner capable of connecting commercial thinking, brand, communications, creative delivery, and technology.

Romania is the primary initial market, with international and broader European work remaining welcome. The default website language will remain English because the intended Romanian audience is internationally literate and comfortable with English branding and marketing terminology. Romanian must closely match the intent and quality of the English version but must be adapted naturally, not translated word for word.

ALFable operates as an expert-led studio. Alexandru personally leads strategy and delivery and can directly provide:

- positioning and brand strategy;
- identity and branding;
- naming and messaging;
- packaging;
- communications and PR direction;
- social media and content systems;
- media planning and buying;
- websites, applications, software, and other digital experiences.

Trusted specialist collaborators extend the team when a project requires photography, film, advanced production, or other specific craft. The website must make this model clear without implying a large permanent agency team.

## 3. Objectives

### Primary objectives

1. Make the right visitor understand within five seconds that ALFable helps founders and owner-led businesses launch, reposition, and build compelling brands and experiences.
2. Present Alexandru's combination of senior strategic judgment and hands-on creative/technical delivery as the central competitive advantage.
3. Make brand, positioning, and customer experience the visible center of the offer.
4. Demonstrate relevance to premium and founder-led businesses even before a complete public client portfolio exists.

6. Establish a visual and commercial position that is relevant to premium-oriented founders and owner-led businesses without narrowing ALFable to fashion, interiors, or any single sector.

### Secondary objectives

- Retain ALFable's ability to attract digital-product and bespoke-software work.

- Do not lead with AI.

- Do not migrate the site to a framework solely for design convenience.

Fashion, interiors, lifestyle, hospitality, beauty, wellness, culture, professional services, premium retail, founder brands, specialist manufacturing, and technology-enabled services.

## 5. Positioning foundation

### Category

**Independent brand and digital studio**

Use "studio" because it signals craft, creation, and direct involvement. Use **expert-led** as the preferred qualifier. Do not alternate between expert-led and founder-led in public copy. Avoid using "consultancy" as the only category because it underplays implementation. Avoid using "agency" as the primary category because it suggests a different operating model and heavier layers.

### Positioning statement

> For founders creating what is next and owner-led businesses ready for their next chapter, ALFable is the independent brand and digital studio that turns ambition into a coherent position, identity, and customer experience. Every engagement combines senior strategic judgment with hands-on creative and technical delivery, led by one accountable partner and extended through trusted specialists when needed.

### Core promise

> From the first business decision to the experience customers actually use.

### Central differentiator

Most providers operate primarily in one part of the problem:

- strategic advisors recommend;
- creative studios express;
- communications agencies promote;
- developers build.

ALFable connects these decisions. The brand proposition, identity, language, campaign, website, application, and supporting systems can be shaped as one coherent experience rather than passed between disconnected suppliers.

### Reasons to believe

- 23+ years of experience across agencies, European/global leadership, and independent creation;
- direct senior involvement rather than junior account layers;
- proven ability to understand complex businesses and translate them into clear propositions;
- practical ability to create identity, packaging, communications, media, websites, applications, and software;
- international perspective with strong understanding of the Romanian market;
- a trusted network that brings in the right specialist craft for the brief.

### Brand character

ALFable should feel:

- intelligent, but not academic;
- premium, but not precious;
- confident, but not self-important;
- creative, but commercially grounded;
- technical, but human;
- editorial, but not austere;
- international, while remaining relevant and accessible in Romania.

### Competitive context and implementation consequence

SILKEIGHT and Flow Thread Studio are useful market references because they demonstrate demand for brand identity, packaging, creative direction, websites, online shops, content, and ongoing brand support among Romanian fashion, lifestyle, interiors, beauty, and premium-oriented businesses.

Their strongest public advantage is visible proof: extensive project imagery, recognizable sector relevance, and a restrained portfolio-led presentation. Their commercial materials are more deliverable-led, with overlapping service lists, limited explanation of method or success criteria, and digital capability framed mainly around standard website and e-commerce production.

ALFable must not imitate their visual identity, layouts, language, or private commercial materials. The implementation consequence is to compete on a different and more valuable organizing idea:

> Business ambition -> position -> identity -> customer experience -> digital product or system -> market.

The website should therefore:

- lead with client situations and business transformation rather than a long service menu;
- make strategic judgment, creative expression, communications, and technical delivery visibly connected;
- present direct senior involvement and one point of accountability as advantages;
- retain real technical depth beyond standard website production;
- use a distinctive cream, black, monochrome, and pear visual system rather than adopting a generic neutral luxury aesthetic;
- compensate for a developing public portfolio through clarity, real work, verified experience, and original perspective - never simulated client proof.

- respect `prefers-reduced-motion`;
- do not block the headline or harm text contrast;
- do not autoplay audio;

#### AI generation review rules

#### Selection scorecard

Score each proposed image family against the same criteria:

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Relevance to ALFable's positioning | 20 |
| Distinctiveness and editorial quality | 20 |
| Coherence across the complete set | 15 |
| Responsive crop and text-safe space | 15 |
| Compatibility with cream, black, and pear | 10 |
| Credibility without implying client work | 10 |
| Technical quality and absence of artifacts | 10 |

Use a 1-5 rating for each criterion. Calculate the weighted contribution as `rating / 5 x weight`, producing a total score out of 100.

Before scoring, apply these pass/fail gates:

- usable source provenance and a credible path to commercial website use;
- no unacceptable anatomical, material, reflection, text-like, or resolution defects;
- no composition that becomes misleading in its intended page context;
- viable desktop and mobile crops.

Resolve close totals in this order: positioning relevance, coherence of the complete family, responsive crop quality, then source/provenance simplicity.

Document the comparison in `docs/ALFABLE-VISUAL-CANDIDATES.md`, including preview reference, source or provider, intended slot, score, crop notes, status, and rejection reason where applicable. Keep shortlisted and approved assets in `docs/ALFABLE-ASSET-MANIFEST.md`; do not bloat the asset manifest with every rejected experiment. Store local comparison sheets under `output/visual-candidates/` and do not commit them unless Alexandru requests it.

Do not choose the AI route merely because it scores well on individual compositions. The final decision must consider the trust and authenticity of the complete website.
