Cookie policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This page explains the use of cookies and similar technologies on alfable.com.

Essential preference storage

When you choose an option in the cookie banner, the site stores your choice in your browser’s local storage under the key alfable-cookie-preferences. This is used only to remember whether optional services may use storage. It is not used for advertising or profiling.

Google Analytics in consent mode

The Google Analytics tag, using measurement ID G-M775PK8CJ2, is present in the page with analytics storage denied by default. This lets the tag respect your choice from the first page load. If you select “Allow optional”, analytics storage is granted and Google Analytics may use cookies such as _ga to help us understand site usage. If you select “Essential only”, analytics storage remains denied.

Fonts

Typefaces are served directly from this website and load for every visitor. No request is made to Google Fonts or any other third-party font service, and no font-related personal data is shared. This is not affected by your cookie choice.

Change your choice

You can reopen the banner at any time using “Cookie settings” in the site footer. Selecting “Essential only” withdraws optional analytics consent for future activity and removes Google Analytics cookies available to this site. You can also clear local storage through your browser settings. Clearing it will cause the banner to appear again when you return.
